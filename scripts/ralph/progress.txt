## Codebase Patterns
- anomaly_detector.py flags use `col` (0-based index) for single-cell highlighting and `full_row: True` for full-row highlighting
- sheets_output.py `_apply_anomaly_highlight` dispatches on `flag.get("full_row")` vs `flag.get("col")`
- Color mapping: high=red(1,0.8,0.8), medium=orange(1,0.9,0.7), low=yellow(1,1,0.7)
- Full-row range uses A{row}:AB{row} (28 columns)
- UNKNOWN_ACCOUNT constant is defined in config.py as "未確定勘定"
